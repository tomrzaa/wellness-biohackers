FAQ

What is hydrogen water?

Hydrogen water is water infused with molecular hydrogen gas (H₂). H₂ is colourless and tasteless and can dissolve into water. Research is ongoing; results may vary.

How do hydrogen water bottles work?

Most use electrolysis (often SPE/PEM) to generate hydrogen that dissolves into the water. Cycle times vary by device.

What matters most when comparing bottles?

Verified output (not just self-claims), build materials, warranty/support, and value for money — especially for NZ buyers (shipping + after-sales support).
