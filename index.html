Frequently Asked Questions

What is hydrogen water?
Hydrogen water is regular water infused with molecular hydrogen (H₂). Hydrogen is a tasteless, odorless gas that can dissolve into water. Research is ongoing and results vary by person.
How do hydrogen water bottles work?
Most bottles use electrolysis (often SPE/PEM) to generate hydrogen. A current passes through water at the electrode assembly, producing hydrogen that dissolves into the water during a cycle.
What matters most when comparing bottles?
Look for verified hydrogen output (ppm/ppb), materials (glass vs plastic), warranty and money-back policies, replacement parts/accessories, ease of cleaning, and clear documentation of claims.
Are ppm/ppb claims always reliable?
Not always. Some brands publish self-reported numbers without independent verification. Treat very high claims skeptically unless accompanied by credible testing documentation.
